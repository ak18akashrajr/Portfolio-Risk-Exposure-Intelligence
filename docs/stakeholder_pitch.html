The Solution

MCP is the
universal plug

Model Context Protocol (MCP) is an open standard — like USB-C, but for AI. It lets any AI assistant connect to your platform and call its analytics capabilities in real time, with no custom integration work for each AI tool.

Think of it this way: Your platform is a brilliant analyst locked in a room. MCP opens the door — so any AI can walk in, ask questions, and get real, live answers from your data.
